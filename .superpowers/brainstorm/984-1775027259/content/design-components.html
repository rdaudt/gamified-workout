AI layer
(on device)
🦴
Pose Detection
Tracks 33 body landmarks in real time using the camera. Draws the skeleton overlay. Runs entirely in the browser — nothing is uploaded.
MediaPipe Pose (Google)
🔢
Rep Counter
Measures joint angles (elbow, hip, knee) to detect full vs. partial reps. Each exercise has its own angle thresholds.
Custom rules per exercise type
📐
Form Analyser
Flags alignment issues: back sagging, knees caving, shallow depth. Records which reps had form breaks. Generates post-session feedback text.
Angle + symmetry thresholds
rep data sent to server (no video)
Effort engine
(server)
📊
History Analyser
Compares today's result against all previous sessions for this exercise. Detects plateaus, streak length, and rate of improvement over time.
⚡
Effort Score
A single score (1–100) that reflects the real difficulty of today's improvement — not just the raw %. A plateau-breaking +3% scores higher than an easy +40%.
✍️
Quote Generator
Generates the "past self" quote calibrated to the context: breakthrough, first win, plateau, consistency streak. Uses AI. Tone is always dark and dramatic.
Claude API
stored in database
Data layer
💾
Workout History
One record per session: exercise, date, rep count, form score, effort score. Small, private, never includes video or camera data.
🃏
Card Store
Generated cards stored as images. Linked to the user's session. Shareable via URL or download. Your branding baked in.
🔔
CTA Engine
Monitors form flags and plateau patterns. Triggers personalised coaching CTAs when thresholds are met. Points to your booking link.
